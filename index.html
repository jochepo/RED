Red

Red is a Pokémon trainer from Kanto region's Palet town. As a young boy, red started his journey to become a Pokemon master, picking Charmander as his first partner. He traveled far and wide, acomplishing many things on his travesy.

Red's accomplishments:

  1. Collected all badges from the Kanto region
  2. Defeated Giovanni and caused the disbandment of Team Rocket
  3. Made his way through the Elite Four and became champion
  4. Completed teh Kanto region's pokedex, capturing all 151 original Pokémon
